HI, I'M DANIEL NGUY

I graduated from the University of California, San Diego in 2013 with a degree in Biology. I soon realized after graduating that this career path was not for me. I've always had a passion for computers and technology so I decided to enroll in a web development bootcamp called LearningFuze. I dedicated the next three months learning how to program in a work-like enviornment for 5 days a week, 12 hours a day. I am now able to build web applications from the ground up utilizing HTML, CSS, and Javascript on the front-end and PHP/MySQL on the back-end. I am committed to continue expanding my knowledge in web development and honing in on my current skills.
